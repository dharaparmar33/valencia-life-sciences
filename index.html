About Us

A focused API manufacturer, built for regulatory rigor.

Valencia Life Sciences is a WHO-GMP certified manufacturer and global exporter of Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients, based in Rajkot, Gujarat, India. We specialize in local anesthetic and anti-inflammatory APIs, built to IP, BP, EP, and USP pharmacopoeial standards.

Since 2020, our combined manufacturing capacity has grown to 108+ MT per month, supplying pharmaceutical companies across 40+ countries with consistent quality and full regulatory documentation.

  • WHO-GMP certified manufacturing for Lidocaine HCl, Lidocaine Base, and Meloxicam
  • DMF filed for Lidocaine HCl and Lidocaine Base, with COPP available
  • Active regulatory filings: South Korea MFDS, China NMPA, EU Written Confirmation
  • Products manufactured to IP / BP / EP / USP pharmacopoeial grade
